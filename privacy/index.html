Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What Cadence Collects

When you use Cadence, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account.
  • Name (optional) — displayed on your profile within the app.
  • Run data — distance, duration, and feel rating for each run you log. All run data is entered manually by you.
  • Journal entries — text you write or dictate after a run. If you use voice journaling, speech recognition runs entirely on your device using Apple's built-in engine. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server. Only the transcribed text is saved.
  • Push notification token — used to send you training reminders on the days you choose.
  • Basic diagnostics — crash reports and app performance data to help us fix bugs.

What We Don't Collect

Cadence does not collect:

  • GPS or location data (the app does not track your location)
  • Health data from Apple Health or HealthKit
  • Contacts, photos, or browsing history
  • Financial or payment information

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Store your run logs and journal entries so you can access them across sessions
  • Send push notifications on your training days (only if you enable them)
  • Improve app stability through anonymised crash reports

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

Third-Party Services

Cadence uses the following services to operate:

  • Supabase — for secure database storage and user authentication. Supabase processes your email and run data on cloud servers. See Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Expo — for delivering app updates and push notifications. Expo receives your push notification token. See Expo's privacy policy.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely via Supabase for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data (profile, runs, journal entries, training plan) is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Your Rights

You can:

  • View your data within the app at any time (your runs, journal, profile)
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen in the app
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us at support@onyourright.in
  • Correct your information by editing your profile in the app

For users in India: your rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) are respected. You may contact us to exercise your rights as a data principal.

Children's Privacy

Cadence is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may notify you through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

support@onyourright.in
